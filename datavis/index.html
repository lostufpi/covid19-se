COVID19 Software Engineering

Pedro Almir Oliveira, Pedro Santos Neto, Gleison Silva, Irvayne Ibiapina, Werney Luz, Rossana M. C. Andrade

This work analyzed eHealth discussions from Stack Overflow (SO) to identify trends and demands based on the developers' perspective. Using the KDD (Knowledge Discovery in Databases) process, we got eHealth questions from the SO database. Then, we processed the data to apply the LDA (Latent Dirichlet Allocation) topic modeling algorithm. We identified 1,076 discussions and 19 meaningful topics. The most discussed topics include the manipulation of medical images, the electronic health record with the HL7 (Health Level 7) standard, and the use of frameworks to support mobile health (mHealth) development. Concerning the demands, we highlight the lack of understanding about the DICOM (Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine) and HL7 structures, the absence of data repositories for testing, and the monitoring of health data in the background for mobile and wearable devices. We concluded that the number of discussions has grown mainly on mHealth, but that there are still opportunities to improve the techniques and processes of this area.
